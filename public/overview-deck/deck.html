COMMUNICATION LAYER
Communication Layer – How information flows
HR FINE-TUNE · FOR AWARENESS
Every decision lands — and every site is heard.
The playbook already pushes decisions down. The feedback gap is the line back up — so we add one trusted source, the “why” behind each call, and a real return path.
How we operate
Decisions flow down → Questions & concerns flow up → Nothing lost → No site left guessing
Four moves that close the Comms Control Gap (2.9) between TE and every acquired site
1Own one source of truth
One owner, one place for every decision and answer — so no site runs on rumor.
2Explain every decision
What was decided, why, and what's next — on a set cadence.
3Open the line back up
Sites send questions, concerns and local strengths up early — so they shape decisions.
4Close every loop
Answer every question and show how input shaped the call — building trust.
What HR Owns
One Channel → Decision Log → Return Path → Enablement
How HR Tracks It
Adoption → Time-to-Answer → Loops Closed → Retention
Employee Outcome
Know What's Happening → Understand Why → Feel Heard → Trust the Direction
Business Outcome
Faster Alignment → Fewer Surprises → Less Friction → Earlier Value

CULTURE LAYER
Culture Layer – How employees experience TE
HR FINE-TUNE · FOR AWARENESS
Culture integration is an employee experience journey.
We started by asking: “How do we help employees understand TE?”
We ended up discovering: Employees join a culture by finding answers to the questions that matter.
1What's Happening?
  • What happened?
  • Why were we acquired?
  • Why now?
  • What will change?
2How Does This Work?
  • Why so many stakeholders?
  • Why are decisions made differently?
  • Why does work feel more complex?
  • Who decides?
3Do I Belong?
  • Do I fit here?
  • Will my voice be heard?
  • Does my experience still matter?
  • Who can help me navigate?
4How Can I Contribute?
  • How can I create value?
  • How can I make an impact?
  • How do we win together?
  • What can TE learn from us?

  • Integration 1:1 Check-in
  • Strategy Alignment
  • Growth & Development
  • Quick Win Opportunities
  • Recognition & etc.
