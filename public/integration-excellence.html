Buddy & Mentor Program

Pair newly acquired employees with experienced TE veterans. This program accelerates cultural integration, provides informal Q&A avenues, and boosts early retention.

Critical Alignment Ensure that mentors from TE are trained on the specific cultural nuances of the acquired organization to prevent early miscommunications.
  • Mentor Selection Criteria: High performers with at least 3 years at TE.
  • Buddy Pairing Logistics: Assign buddies 1 week prior to Day 1.
  • Conversation Guides: Scripts for the first coffee chat, 30-day sync, and 60-day check-in.
